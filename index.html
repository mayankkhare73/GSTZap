How it works

From invoice to return
in four steps

No accounting knowledge needed. No complicated setup. Upload and GSTZap handles the rest.

1

Upload your data

Drag and drop your sales & purchase files — Excel, CSV, Tally XML, or e-invoice JSON. We auto-detect the format.

2

Auto-reconcile ITC

GSTZap fetches your GSTR-2B and matches it against your purchase register. Mismatches are flagged instantly.

3

Review & approve

See a clean summary of your GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B. Fix any flagged issues with one click before generating.

4

Export or file

Download the portal-ready JSON file or a CA-ready PDF report. One-click GSP filing coming in Phase 2.
